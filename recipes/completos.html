Completos

Description

"Completos" is a premium Chilean dish, it is in essence a hotdog, with a million posibilities for toppings. In this recipe we will make a "Completo Italiano" the most common form of the Completo.

Ingredients:

  • Hotdog buns (6 units)
  • Hotdogs or sausages (6 units)
  • A ripe avocado
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Mayonaisse, mustard or ketchup to finish it off
  • A teaspoon of sunflower or olive oil

Steps:

  1. Put the oil in a broad pan, when it is hot, add the hotdogs and cook until they become roasted.
  2. Grab the hotdog buns and remove a little dough from the inside, we do this to make room for the other ingredients. Put them in the oven for 5 minutes.
  3. With a knife, cut the avocado in half, take out the insides, put them in a bowl or plate, and mash them. Add a little salt.
  4. Peel and dice the tomatoes, as small as possible. Add a little oil and salt.
  5. Put the hotdogs in the buns, then add the diced tomatoes on top, and the avocado paste on top of that. Finish it with copious amounts of Mayo, Mustard and/or Ketchup
  6. Enjoy! Here is some ideas for more toppings: Fried onions, chips, bacon, melted cheese. Completos are very versatile and tasty!
