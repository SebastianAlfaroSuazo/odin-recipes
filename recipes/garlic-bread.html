Garlic Bread

Description

This wonderful meal, is very tasty and very easy to make. You can eat it as an appetizer or as your whole meal, depending on the quantity.

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 200 grs Parmesan or Mozarella Cheese
  • Oregano
  • Cilantro
  • 50 grs Butter
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Slice the baguette in smaller buns.
  3. Dice the garlic cloves.
  4. Spread butter on the bread, add diced garlic on top.
  5. Grate the cheese, and sprinkle it on top of your bread.
  6. On top of that add oregano, cilantro and salt at your discretion.
  7. Transfer to baking sheet, cook for 10 min or until the cheese melts.
  8. Enjoy!
